About Me

this picture looks quite dramatic, but i promise i dont take myself *that* seriously, its just a nice photo of me

Hello! I'm Arunim, a student at the University of Pennsylvania studying Physics, maybe some CS, Philosophy, and/or Math. I'm quite interested in effective altruism, in the sense of maximizing good/wellbeing while minimizing suffering. Current areas of interest: AI alignment, global poverty, climate adaptation, policy, and forecasting. As of now, these are quite broad, but I'll narrow them down soon. Generally aiming for utopia in the long run!

Outside of school, I absolutely love to practice sleight-of-hand, run moderately long distances, and test my creative limits at makerspaces (check out some of my favorite projects). My free time is most often devoted to these passions, as well as to reading Calvin and Hobbes or watching/reading the Adventures of Tintin. I also spend some time working to end sexual violence, as a member of ASAP at Penn.

Work Experience

Material Science Intern | Holy Grail (website)

Tackled modular direct air carbon capture device technology at a startup during seed round stage ($2.7M raised). Supported CEO and CTO to streamline and set up the laboratory, identified key materials with desired properties for testing, and secured specialized materials and precision lab equipment from suppliers.

Mentor | MakeX Palo Alto (website)

Volunteered as a mentor in a student-run makerspace, helping community members of all ages create projects of their choosing as well as keeping the space functioning as a whole. Taught interested middle and high school students how to design and fabricate with project based learning. Managed budget, outreach, and publicity, and mentored during open hours.

Paid Intern | Stanford University Geophysics

Conducted field work and data analysis involving underground fiber optic cables using Distributed Acoustic Sensing (DAS) to improve seismic monitoring by collecting large amounts of data and mapping the cable to geographical coordinates.

Camp Counselor | City of Palo Alto Recreation

Worked at outdoor summer camps based in Foothills Park, oversaw and ran activities for about 50 kids.
